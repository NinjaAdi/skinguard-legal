What Skinwise does

📸

Skin Scans

Take a photo of a mole or spot. The AI analyzes it using the ABCDE method dermatologists use and explains what it observed in plain language.

🧴

Product Checks

Scan an ingredient label. See which ingredients are flagged by safety databases, what they do, and simpler alternatives worth considering.

☀️

UV & Weather

Live UV index, temperature, humidity, and wind for your location — with a sunscreen strength recommendation matched to the conditions. Check today or tomorrow.

📋

Care Plans

Build daily routines from your scan results. Set reminders, track streaks, and see how your skin changes over time.

💬

AI Advisor

Ask questions in normal language about your results, ingredients, sun safety, or when to see a dermatologist. Answers draw from AAD, NCI, and WHO sources.

🎨

Five Themes

Porcelain & Sage, Warm Clay, Rose, Violet, or Onyx & Champagne dark mode. Your phone's system theme never overrides your choice.

🔒

Privacy, built in

Your photos are analyzed and never stored on our servers. Scan history, care plans, and your profile stay on your device. Location is used only in the moment for weather — never saved. No ads, no tracking, no data sales. Delete everything anytime, in the app or on the web.

AI disclosure: analyses and advisor responses in Skinwise are generated by artificial intelligence. AI output is educational information, not medical advice, and can be wrong. Nothing in the app replaces examination by a qualified healthcare professional.

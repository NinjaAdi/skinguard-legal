Privacy Policy

For the SkinGuard AI mobile application ("the App"), operated by SkinGuard LLC ("we," "us," "our").
Last updated: June 25, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The App is an educational tool only and is not a medical device. By using the App you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a password. Your password is never stored in readable form — it is protected using one-way cryptographic hashing (PBKDF2). Account information is stored on our secure backend hosted by Cloudflare.

Photos you submit

When you scan a skin area or a product, the photo is sent securely to our backend and then to our AI processing provider (Anthropic) to generate an educational assessment. Photos are processed only to produce your result and are not stored on our servers. A small copy (thumbnail) of your scans is kept only on your own device as part of your scan history, which you can delete at any time.

Location

If you grant permission, the App uses your device location momentarily to retrieve the local UV index and weather for sun-safety guidance. This location is used at the time of the request and is not stored by us. Location is sent to weather (Open-Meteo) and map/geocoding (OpenStreetMap/Nominatim) services to return local conditions.

Information stored on your device

Your scan history, care plans, profile preferences, and sign-in session are stored locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not collected by us.

Health information

Profile details you optionally provide (such as skin-related or demographic information) are stored only on your device to personalize educational guidance and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in.
  • To generate educational assessments of skin photos and product ingredients.
  • To show local UV/weather for sun-safety education.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share the minimum data needed with providers who process it on our behalf:

  • Anthropic — AI processing of submitted photos/text to generate assessments.
  • Cloudflare — secure hosting of our backend and account data.
  • Open-Meteo — weather/UV data from location coordinates.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — converts coordinates to a place name.

These providers act as our service providers and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

4. Data Security

Data transmitted between the App and our backend is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed. Access to backend data is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Delete individual scans at any time within the App.
  • Delete your entire account and associated data from within the App (Settings → Delete Account).
  • Request account and data deletion from outside the App by emailing skin.guard.llc@gmail.com or visiting our deletion page at skin.guard.llc@gmail.com.
  • Revoke camera or location permission at any time in your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU) or CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years of age or older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account is active and deleted when you delete your account. Submitted photos are not retained on our servers after processing. On-device data remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notifying you in the App.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Policy or your data: skin.guard.llc@gmail.com, SkinGuard LLC, Texas, USA.

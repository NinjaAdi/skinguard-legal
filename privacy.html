We tell you this clearly because we believe you should always know when AI â€” not a person â€” is producing information about your health.

What this means for you:

  • AI output is educational information, not medical advice. The AI does not diagnose, treat, or screen for any condition, and it can be wrong. Nothing in the app replaces examination by a qualified healthcare professional.
  • No automated decisions are made about you. AI output in Skinwise is informational only. It is never used to grant or deny you access to any service, price, or opportunity, and no human or automated system acts on it besides showing it to you.
  • No AI training on your data. Photos and messages are sent to the AI service solely to generate your result. Under our API arrangement, they are not used to train AI models.
  • Human oversight is yours. We encourage you to treat every result as a starting point for a conversation with a dermatologist or physician, especially for anything changing, growing, bleeding, or worrying you.

7. Security

All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are stored only as salted PBKDF2 hashes. Sessions use signed, expiring tokens. Sign-in is protected by rate limiting and automatic lockout after repeated failures. Photos are never written to server storage or logs. Health content stays on your device. No system is perfectly secure, but if a breach affecting your data ever occurs, we will notify you and regulators as required by law, including the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule.

8. Children

Skinwise is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Data retention

Account records are kept until you delete your account. Photos are not retained on servers at all. On-device data persists until you delete it, delete your account, or uninstall the app. Server-side rate-limit and lockout counters expire automatically within minutes.
